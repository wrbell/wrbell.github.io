About

I'm a mechanical engineering and computer science student at the University of Michigan — Dearborn, graduating December 2026. My work sits at the intersection of physical systems and software — building tools that bridge hardware and code.

I've spent four internships at SpaceX across Starship launch, space lasers, and Starfactory automation, plus a controls stint at Tesla's 4680 battery line. These experiences shaped how I think about manufacturing systems: robust, automated, and instrumented.

Outside of industry, I lead research in additive manufacturing at UM-Dearborn's Pannier Lab, race electric vehicles with Dearborn Electric Racing, and build open-source tools for problems I encounter — from real-time translation to FEA workflows.
